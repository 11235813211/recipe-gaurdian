Mousse au Chocolate

12 oz semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 pint cream
4 eggs
1/4 cup water
1 tbsp butter

Separate the 4 eggs. Whisk the 4 egg yolks and 1/4 cup water over medium-low heat.
Melt the 12 oz chocolate and 1 tbsp butter in the yolk mixture.
Let the chocolate cool.
Beat the pint of cream until fully whipped.
Beat the egg whites until stiff.
With the cooled chocolate, fold around 1/3 of the chocolate into the whipped cream. Then fold in the rest.
Fold in the egg whites.
